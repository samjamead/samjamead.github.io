A bit more about me

I write copy and build websites for clients of all shapes and sizes —

This year I've been working with a marine conservation charity, an NFT artist, a group of healthcare professionals, and an electrical engineer.

I work with whatever tools, technology, or platform is best suited to each project or client: from vanilla JavaScript to React, and from Jamstack to SquareSpace, Shopify, and Wordpress.
