Hello – I'm Sam, a physics teacher in Cornwall. I share things I'm working on, which at the moment is mainly things to do with education in the UK. In particular I'm interested in the big issues facing education.

Before teaching I lived in Perth, Western Australia, and worked in web development, marketing, and startups. Technically I'm an oceanographer by training, but haven't used those skills in practice. Yet.

I'm interested in social justice, the environment, and politics. Surfing, skiing, and running. And Oxford commas, golf, and woodwork. Reading. And languages. I have too many hobbies.
