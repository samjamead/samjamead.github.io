Hello! 👋

I'm Sam, a physics teacher in Cornwall.

I share things I'm working on, which at the moment is mainly things to do with education in the UK. In particular I'm interested in the big issues facing education.

Every week I also summarise a journal article in education research — you can find them piling up over at 52papers.org.

Before teaching, I lived in Perth, Western Australia, and worked in web development and marketing. And before that I cofounded a startup called Saberr. Technically I'm an oceanographer by training, but haven't used those skills in practice. Yet.

I'm interested in social justice, the environment, and politics. Surfing, skiing, and running. And Oxford commas, golf, and woodwork. Reading. And languages.

I have too many hobbies.
